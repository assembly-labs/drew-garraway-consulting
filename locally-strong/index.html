What We Do

Three pillars guide our work in building stronger local economies.

Connect

Building bridges between local producers, businesses, and the communities they serve through markets, events, and partnerships.

Educate

Providing resources, mentorship, and hands-on workshops to help small businesses develop the skills they need to thrive.

Grow

Creating sustainable economic growth that keeps dollars local and benefits the entire community for generations to come.
