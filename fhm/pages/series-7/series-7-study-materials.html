Study Approach

The reality: 73% of this exam is in Section 3. Options alone can be a quarter of your questions. If you're not comfortable with options max gain/loss/breakeven calculations, you're not ready.

The strategy: Master calculations first. The formula sheet exists for a reason— these need to be automatic. Then layer in the conceptual stuff. Know the "why" behind regulations, not just the "what."

Time investment: Plan for 100-150 hours over 8-10 weeks. That's 2-3 hours daily. Front-load Section 3 content.
