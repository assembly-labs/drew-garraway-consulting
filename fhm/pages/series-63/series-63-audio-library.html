Chapters 1, 2 & 7

Who Registers Where: The Series 63 Registration Framework

~22 min Broker-Dealers, Agents & IARs

The three chapters of the Series 63 that answer one question: who has to register, and where? Covers the USA §401(c) broker-dealer definition, the 15-offer de minimis safe harbor, noon of the 30th day, the 11 grounds for denial under §204, agent registration and the five §402(a) issuer-agent exemptions, Form U4/U5/U6 (firm, firm, regulator), FINRA Rule 3210 outside accounts, FINRA Rule 2040 payments, and the NSMIA compromise that gives states jurisdiction over IARs even when the firm is SEC-registered.

Chapters 3 & 8

Doing the Wrong Thing and What Happens Next: Ethics and Remedies

~28 min 36% of the exam

Ethics is 25% of the exam. Remedies is 11%. Together they are 36% of the Series 63 in one episode. Covers the NASAA Dishonest Practices Statement of Policy (including the brand-new April 2025 "adviser" title rule), the 10-business-day oral discretion rule for investment advisers, the 0.25% no-load ceiling, the Vulnerable Adults Model Act 2/7/15/25 timing ladder, Regulation Best Interest and D-C-C-C, the DOL fiduciary rule that the Fifth Circuit vacated in 2018, the 5% policy that is a guide not a rule, the Uniform Prudent Investor Act and 164 years of precedent, Section 101 antifraud with no exemptions, Administrator powers and limits, Section 409 criminal penalties, Section 410 civil recovery, the known §410(e) statute-of-limitations discrepancy, the rescission window, judicial review, service of process, and SIPC.
