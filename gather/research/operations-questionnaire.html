Operations Questionnaire · 18 questions · ~15 minutes

Hey Carlo & Dara,

Your ops playbook is the real product — the software just scales it. These questions dig into how you actually run Berwyn so we can build the right tools.

Answer however works best: fill out below, download the Word doc, or copy the questions into an email.

— Drew

1

Vendor Management

How you onboard vendors, manage rotation, and track compliance.

1. Walk me through how a new vendor joins Berwyn — from first inquiry to first market day. What documents do you require?

2. You have 70+ vendors with 25-30 selling weekly. How do you manage that rotation? What determines who gets a spot on any given Sunday?

3. How do you assign stall locations? First-come, seniority, product type, or something else?

4. How do you track vendor compliance (insurance, licenses, certifications)? What happens when something expires?

2

Fees & Payments

How you charge vendors and collect payment.

5. What's your fee structure? (Daily flat fee? Seasonal fee? Percentage of sales? A mix?)

6. How and when do vendors pay you? What happens when someone is late?

3

Vendor Communication

How you keep vendors informed and handle changes.

7. How do you communicate with vendors? (Email, text, WhatsApp, phone?) What's the typical cadence?

8. How do you handle weather cancellations or last-minute changes? How much notice do vendors need?

4

Customer Communication

Your Main Line customers value convenience and personal touch. How do you stay connected?

9. You text customers directly — walk me through what messages you send. Order confirmations? Pickup reminders? "What's fresh this week"?

10. How do you manage customer texts right now? Personal phones? Shared number? What's working and what's painful?

11. If you could send automated messages to customers (reminders, updates, availability alerts), what would be most valuable?

5

Order Fulfillment & Admin Operations

The behind-the-scenes work — print sheets, packing lists, pickup management. This is core.

12. Walk me through the order-to-pickup process — from when a customer places an order to when they drive away with their bags.

13. What print materials do you create? Packing lists for vendors? Customer order sheets? Pickup checklists? Walk me through what gets printed and who uses it.

14. How do vendors receive their orders? Email? Printed sheets you hand them? How do they know what to pack?

15. What's the most time-consuming admin task in the order fulfillment process? Where do you and your team spend the most hours?

6

Looking Ahead

What's missing, what would help, and anything else we should know.

16. Your customers value convenience. What do they ask for that you currently can't provide?

17. If we could automate ONE thing about your current operations, what would save you the most time?

18. Anything else about how you run Berwyn that we should know before we start building?
