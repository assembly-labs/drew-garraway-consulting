Gather Questionnaire

10 questions, ~10 minutes

Hey Carlo & Dara,

Thanks for taking 10 minutes to help shape Gather. We've done our research, but you're the real experts.

These questions focus on what we can't learn from documentation: your priorities, your frustrations, and what would actually make your life easier.

— Drew

1. What's your single biggest operational headache right now?

The thing that wastes the most time or causes the most stress.

2. If you could wave a magic wand and fix ONE thing about how you run the market, what would it be?

3. What tools or systems have you tried that didn't work? Why did they fail?

Software, apps, processes, workarounds that didn't stick.

4. Describe your least tech-savvy vendor. What would it take for them to adopt a new system?

This helps us design for the hardest case, not the easiest.

5. Walk me through a "bad day" at the market. What goes wrong?

The chaotic mornings, the vendor no-shows, the customer complaints.

6. What do customers ask for that you currently can't provide?

7. What's the #1 thing that would make your vendors happier?

8. How much time do you spend on admin work each week? What takes the longest?

Be specific: "3 hours on X, 2 hours on Y..."

9. What would make you trust a new platform enough to actually use it?

What would overcome your skepticism?

10. Anything else we should know?
