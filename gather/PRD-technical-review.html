1. Executive Summary

What We're Building

A web-based marketplace that lets customers:

  1. Browse all products from a farmers market's vendors
  2. Add items from multiple vendors to a single cart
  3. Checkout with one payment
  4. Pick up their order at the market

Why It Matters

StakeholderCurrent PainOur Solution
Customers Can only shop Saturday 8am–1pm; don't know what's available Shop online anytime; see real inventory
Vendors Miss 51% of potential customers who can't attend Reach everyone; predictable pre-orders
Markets Can't compete with grocery delivery apps Modern shopping experience with market pickup
Key Architectural Decision: No Delivery Gather is pickup-only. No last-mile delivery. This is a deliberate decision based on the catastrophic failures of Good Eggs ($200M+ burned), Farmigo ($26M burned), and Webvan ($800M bankruptcy)—all of which proved last-mile fresh food delivery economics are fundamentally broken.

2. The Problem & Market Opportunity

Catherine's Story

Catherine lives 5 minutes from Berwyn Farmers Market. She wants to support Tom's Farm down the road instead of Whole Foods. But she works Saturday mornings, doesn't know if heirloom tomatoes will be in stock, and by the time she arrives at 11am, the good stuff is gone. She can't pre-order anything.

Result: Catherine opens Instacart and orders from Whole Foods. She feels guilty, but convenience wins.

3. The Solution

Core Value Proposition

“Shop local from your couch. Pick up fresh at the market.”

How It Works

Thursday Evening (Catherine at home)
├── Opens gather.market/berwyn
├── Browses: Produce → Tomatoes → Heirloom
├── Adds items from 5 different vendors
├── Checks out once ($75 total)
└── Selects pickup: Saturday 11am-12pm

Saturday Morning (Behind the scenes)
├── Vendors receive pick lists
├── Staff collects items from each vendor
└── Orders packed and labeled with QR codes

Saturday 11:30am (Catherine at market)
├── Shows QR code at pickup tent
├── Grabs her bag
└── Done in 3 minutes
MVP Note: Gather Team Manages Products In MVP, the Gather team manages all products on behalf of vendors. Vendors do not log in or manage their own listings. Vendor self-service is Phase 2.

Market Staff

2–4 people • Moderate tech comfort • Busy on market day
“I need to see all orders at a glance and check people in fast.”

Jobs: See pending orders, generate pick lists, check in customers, handle issues.

Success: Pick list accuracy 99%, check-in <30s, zero lost orders.

Tertiary — Admin

Admin (Carlo / Gather Team)

Manages products on behalf of 70+ vendors in MVP
“I need to add products, toggle availability, and see basic reports.”

Jobs: Add/edit products with photos, toggle availability, set pickup times, view sales reports.

F3: Checkout & Payment

Goal: Single-page checkout. Guest checkout. Stripe payments (card + Apple Pay + Google Pay). Pickup time slot selection.

FeatureDescription

Status managementPending → Packed → Picked Up → Complete. Issue flags separate from status.
Quick stats headerOrders today, packed count, picked up count, issues count.
Bulk actionsMulti-select orders for bulk “Mark Packed” or “Generate Pick Lists”.

Full spec: docs/OPERATIONS_DASHBOARD_SPEC.md

F6: Pick List System

Goal: Generate consolidated, printable pick lists grouped by vendor for market-day order fulfillment.

FeatureDescription
Pick list generatorSelect date + vendors. Consolidated view: same products grouped across orders.
Print-optimized outputB&W, clean margins, checkboxes for packing. Vendor name, date, item counts.
PDF downloadDownloadable PDF per vendor for email distribution.
Bulk printOne-click to print all vendor pick lists.

F7: Customer Check-in

Goal: Fast, reliable customer pickup processing via QR code scanning.

FeatureDescription
QR scannerCamera-based scanner. Shows order summary on successful scan.
Manual fallbackSearch by order #, customer name, phone, or email when QR fails.
Complete pickupOne-tap to mark order picked up. Recent check-ins list.
Error statesAlready picked up, cancelled, wrong date, not packed warnings.

F8: Issue Resolution

Goal: Handle unavailable items, substitutions, and refunds gracefully.

FeatureDescription
Mark item unavailableFlag item with resolution: refund, substitute, or contact customer.
Substitution flowSearch/select replacement. Auto-handle price differences (refund if cheaper).
Refund processingFull or partial refunds via Stripe. Reason codes. Customer email notification.
No-show handlingEnd-of-day view of unpicked orders. Contact actions, hold, or refund.

F9: Admin Product Management

Goal: Gather team manages 500+ products across 70+ vendors efficiently.

FeatureDescription
Product CRUDCreate/edit/delete products. Fields: name, vendor, category, price, unit type, description, photo, dietary tags.
Photo uploadDrag-and-drop with auto-compression (max 1MB, 1200px). WebP conversion. Thumbnails.
Availability managementBinary toggle (In Stock / Out of Stock). Quick toggle from list view. Bulk toggle per vendor.
Vendor managementCRUD for vendor profiles: name, photo, description, contact, active status.
Category managementCRUD for categories. Drag-and-drop reordering. Predefined dietary tags.
Search & filtersSearch by name. Filter by vendor, category, availability. Combined filters.

Full spec: docs/ADMIN_FEATURES.md

F10: Admin Market Configuration

FeatureDescription
Market profileName, logo, address, description, contact info.
Pickup configurationMarket days, hours, time slots, max orders per slot, order cutoff time.
Fee configurationService fee type (%, flat, or none) and amount. Optional minimum order.
Staff managementInvite users. Roles: Admin, Staff, Check-in Only.

F11: Payments & Vendor Payouts

Platform: Stripe Connect with Express accounts. Stripe handles KYC, vendor dashboards, 1099-K filing.

FeatureDescription
Customer paymentSingle charge via PaymentIntent. Multi-vendor split data. PCI via Stripe Elements.
Vendor onboardingStripe-hosted Express onboarding. Bank account + identity verification.
Payout calculationVendor share = item sales − platform commission. Refund adjustments.
Weekly payoutsACH transfers every Monday for prior week's completed orders. 2-day hold after pickup.
Refund processingFull/partial/item-level refunds. Deducted from vendor's next payout.
Chargeback handlingWebhook notifications. Evidence collection (QR pickup proof). Platform absorbs costs in MVP.

Refund id, order_id, order_item_id, stripe_refund_id, amount_cents, reason, status Item-level and full-order refunds. Deducted from vendor payout.
User id, email, password_hash, name, role (admin/staff/check_in), market_id Staff authentication only in MVP. No customer accounts.

     ?category=produce&vendor=mitchell-farm&dietary=organic&search=tomatoes
     &sort=popular|price_asc|price_desc&limit=20&offset=0

GET  /api/markets/:slug/pickup-slots?date= → Available slots with capacity
POST /api/markets/:slug/orders             → Create order + process payment
     Body: { items, customer, pickup_slot_id, payment_method_id }
GET  /api/orders/:id?token=abc123          → Order details (token for guests)

PATCH /api/admin/orders/:id                → Update order status
