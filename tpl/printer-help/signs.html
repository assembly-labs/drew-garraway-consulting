Print-Ready Signs

Tredyffrin Public Library

Printing these signs: Use your browser's print function (Ctrl+P or Cmd+P). Each sign will print on a separate page. Designed to be readable from 3-5 feet.

How to Copy

  1. Place your document
    • Feeder: Face UP
    • Glass: Face DOWN
  2. Press Copy
  3. Choose Black or Full Color
  4. Enter number of copies
  5. Press Start
  6. Take your copies and original

Black & White: $0.10/page  |  Color: $0.30/page

Pay at Front Desk

Scan to USB Drive

  1. Insert your USB drive
    • Wait for "Found USB Device"
  2. Press Scan, then tap USB
  3. Place your document
    • Feeder: Face UP
    • Glass: Face DOWN
  4. Press Scan
    • Repeat for more pages
  5. Press Job Finish
  6. WAIT for "USB Device can be removed"
    • Then remove your drive

Scanning is FREE

Scan to Email

  1. Press Scan, then tap E-mail
  2. Enter your email address
  3. Choose PDF as the format
  4. Place your document
    • Feeder: Face UP
    • Glass: Face DOWN
  5. Press Scan
    • Repeat for more pages
  6. Press Job Finish
    • Check your inbox (and spam folder)

Scanning is FREE

Long documents? Use USB instead.

How to Print

At the Computer:

  1. Click Print → Select Cloud Print → Click Print
  2. Note the User ID on the monitor (example: "0001")

At the Printer:

  1. Touch the orange Elevate Sky Print icon
  2. Enter User ID and Password → Touch green Go
  3. Touch the icon with a number in a white circle
  4. Select your document → Touch Print

Pay at Front Desk immediately after printing

Double-Sided Copies

To copy double-sided:

  1. Press Copy
  2. Tap 1-Sided/2-Sided
  3. Choose your option:
    • 1→2 = Single original → Double-sided copy
    • 2→2 = Double original → Double-sided copy
  4. Press Start

Need help? Ask the Reference Desk Librarian.

If Something Goes Wrong

Problem What To Do
Paper Jam STOP. Notify staff.
Blank pages Check document: Feeder = UP, Glass = DOWN
Print job missing Look for number in white circle. No number = not sent.
Scan didn't arrive Check spam folder. Try USB instead.
USB not recognized Remove, wait 5 seconds, reinsert firmly.
Screen frozen Press Home or Function Clear

Don't panic. You can't break it.

Press Stop to cancel any job.

Questions? Ask the Reference Desk Librarian.
