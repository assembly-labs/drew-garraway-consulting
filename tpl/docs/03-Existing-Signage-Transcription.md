## Sign 1: Printing from Public Computer

### Printing from Public Computer

After clicking "Print" or Control+P, follow these steps:

1. Select Printer: "Cloud Print" 
2. Click "Print" 


> **Please note:** If you do not see "Cloud Print," please ask the Reference Librarian for assistance.

3. Make a note of the User ID and Password taped to the bottom of the monitor (ex: "0001")
4. Go over to the Public Printer and follow the steps to release your pages.

## Sign 2: Printing Your Document

### Printing Your Document

1. Touch the orange **Elevate Sky Print** icon
   (Image of orange cloud icon labeled "Elevate Sky Print")

2. Enter the four digit User ID and Password from bottom of monitor (ex: "0001"), then touch the green "Go" button.

3. Touch the icon that has a number in a white circle. (No number means nothing sent.)

4. Touch the name of the document(s) you wish to print and then touch the printer icon.

5. Confirm the print settings then touch "Print".

6. Please pay for copies at the Front Desk immediately after printing
