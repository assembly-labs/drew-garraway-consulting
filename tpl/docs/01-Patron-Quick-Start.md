# Quick Start Guide
## Toshiba e-STUDIO 2525AC Copier/Scanner/Printer

---

## COPYING

**What is copying?** You have a paper document and want another paper copy. The original goes in, and you get printed copies out — all at this machine.

### Black & White Copy
1. Place your document:
   - **Stack of pages:** Put them face **up** in the top feeder (ADF)
   - **Single page or book:** Lift the lid and place it face **down** on the glass
2. Press **Copy** (or **Home** then **Copy**)
3. Tap **Black** for black and white
4. Use the number keys to enter how many copies you need
5. Press the blue **Start** button
6. Take your copies and your original

### Color Copy
1. Place your document (same as above)
2. Press **Copy**
3. Tap **Full Color**
4. Enter the number of copies
5. Press **Start**

### Double-Sided (Duplex) Copy
1. Place your document
2. Press **Copy**
3. Tap **1-Sided/2-Sided**
4. Choose **1→2** (single to double) or **2→2** (double to double)
5. Press **Start**

**To cancel:** Press **Stop**, then confirm

## SCANNING

**What is scanning?** You have a paper document and want to turn it into a digital file (like a PDF). The paper goes in, and you get a file saved to your USB drive or sent to your email.

### Scan to USB Drive
1. Plug your USB drive into the port on the machine
2. Wait for the screen to say **"Found USB Device"**
3. Press **Scan**, then tap **USB** (or **File/USB** then **USB Media**)
4. Tap **File Name** to rename your file (optional)
5. Tap **File Format** and choose **PDF** or **JPEG**
6. Place your document (feeder = face up, glass = face down)
7. Press **Scan**
8. For more pages: add the next page and press **Scan** again
9. When done, press **Job Finish**
10. **Wait** until the screen says **"USB Device can be removed"**
11. Remove your USB drive

### Scan to Email (if available at your library)
1. Press **Scan**, then tap **E-mail**
2. Enter your email address (or select from the address book)
3. Tap **File Format** and choose **PDF**
4. Place your document
5. Press **Scan**
6. When done, press **Job Finish**
7. Check your email inbox (and spam folder)

## PRINTING

**What is printing?** You have a digital file on a computer and want it on paper. You send the file from the computer, then come to this machine to pick up your printed pages.

### Step 1: Send Your Print Job (at the computer)
1. Open your document and click **Print** (or press Control+P)
2. Select **Cloud Print** as your printer
3. Click **Print**
4. Note the **User ID and Password** taped to the bottom of the monitor (example: "0001")

> **Don't see "Cloud Print"?** Ask the Reference Desk Librarian for assistance.

### Step 2: Release Your Print Job (at the printer)
1. Touch the orange **Elevate Sky Print** icon on the screen
2. Enter the **User ID** and **Password** from the computer monitor, then touch the green **Go** button
3. Touch the icon that has a **number in a white circle** (no number = nothing was sent)
4. Touch the name of your document(s), then touch the **printer icon**
5. Confirm your settings (Color, 2-Sided, Copies), then touch **Print**
6. **Pay for your prints at the Front Desk immediately after printing**

### Common Print Problems
| Problem | Try This |
|---------|----------|
| Wrong paper size | Check your document settings before printing |
| Pages are sideways | Change orientation to Portrait or Landscape in print settings |
| No number on the icon | Your job didn't send — go back to the computer and try again |
| Can't find Cloud Print | Ask the Reference Desk Librarian for assistance |

## CHOOSING PAPER SIZE

- **Letter (8.5 x 11):** Standard size, usually in Drawer 1
- **Legal (8.5 x 14):** Longer paper, check which drawer or ask staff

To change paper size when copying:
1. Press **Copy**
2. Tap **Paper**
3. Select the drawer with your paper size

---

## BEFORE YOU LEAVE

- **Take your original document** from the feeder or glass
- **Log out** by pressing **Function Clear** or **End Process**
- **Check for your pages** in the output tray
