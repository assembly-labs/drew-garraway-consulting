# Scout & Finch: Pricing & Demo Strategy

## Executive Summary

This document outlines pricing architecture, demo offerings, and conversion strategies for Scout & Finch. Based on market research showing average library technology budgets of $408,100 with 33% allocated to software beyond ILS (~$135,000), we're positioning our solution at $7,995-$39,995 annually to capture budget without triggering complex procurement.

**Key Decisions Required:**
1. Bundle vs. Separate pricing (Recommendation: Bundle for simplicity)
2. Specific tier pricing within validated ranges
3. Beta/pilot incentive structure
4. Demo delivery approach (Drew personally vs. scaled approach)

## Pricing Architecture Decision

### Option A: Bundle Pricing (Scout + Finch Together) **[RECOMMENDED]**

**Rationale for Bundling:**
- Simpler sales conversation (one product, one price)
- Higher perceived value (comprehensive solution)
- Easier procurement (single PO, single vendor)
- Better competitive positioning vs. point solutions
- Natural upsell from either product to bundle
- Aligns with library preference for integrated solutions

**Bundle Pricing Tiers:**

| Tier | Service Population | Annual Price | What's Included | Target Libraries |
|------|-------------------|--------------|-----------------|------------------|
| **Starter** | <25K patrons | **$7,995** | Scout + Finch Lite (2 staff seats) | Small libraries, rural systems |
| **Professional** | 25K-75K patrons | **$18,995** | Scout + Finch Pro (unlimited seats) | Mid-sized libraries (sweet spot) |
| **Enterprise** | 75K-250K patrons | **$39,995** | Scout + Finch Pro + Premium Support | Large county systems |
| **Strategic** | 250K+ patrons | **Custom** | White-glove implementation | Urban systems (Year 2) |

**Finch Lite vs. Pro Differentiation:**

**Finch Lite (Included in Starter):**
- 2 staff user accounts
- Basic onboarding content (pre-loaded)
- Email support only
- Monthly usage reports
- Standard integrations (ILS + OverDrive)

**Finch Pro (Professional & Enterprise):**
- Unlimited staff accounts
- Custom knowledge base setup
- Phone + email support
- Real-time analytics dashboard
- All integrations (ILS + OverDrive + databases)
- Workflow automation builder
- API access (Enterprise only)

### Option B: Separate Pricing (If Unbundling Required)

**When to Offer Separate Pricing:**
- Library explicitly says "we only want patron-facing"
- Budget constraints require phased adoption
- Consortium wants to test one product first
- Grant funding restricted to specific use case

**Standalone Pricing:**

**Scout Only:**
- Starter: $5,995
- Professional: $12,995
- Enterprise: $24,995

**Finch Add-On:**
- Lite: +$3,000 (any tier)
- Pro: +$7,000 (Professional)
- Pro+: +$15,000 (Enterprise)

**Conversion Strategy:**
- Start with Scout (easier to explain)
- Demo Finch during implementation
- Offer 20% bundle discount to upgrade

## Demo Offering Strategy

### Demo Tier 1: Discovery Call (Qualification)

**Format:** 15-minute phone/video call

**Audience:** Initial contact, not yet qualified

**Content:**
- Understand their challenges (3-5 minutes)
- High-level solution overview (5 minutes)
- Confirm ILS compatibility (2 minutes)
- Schedule deeper demo if qualified (3 minutes)

**Tools Needed:**
- Calendly link for easy scheduling
- Qualification checklist
- One-pager PDF to send after

**Conversion Goal:** 50% to Demo Tier 2

### Demo Tier 2: Personalized Deep Dive **[PRIMARY DEMO]**

**Format:** 45-minute video call with screen share

**Audience:** Qualified prospects (right size, ILS, budget)

**Content Structure:**
1. **Context Setting (5 min)**
   - Confirm attendees and roles
   - Recap their challenges

2. **Scout Demo (15 min)**
   - Live search using THEIR catalog
   - Show patron experience
   - Highlight differentiators

3. **Finch Demo (15 min)**
   - Staff workflow improvements
   - Knowledge base examples
   - ROI calculation specific to them

4. **Implementation Overview (5 min)**
   - Timeline (4-5 weeks)
   - Support structure
   - Success metrics

5. **Pricing & Next Steps (5 min)**
   - Show relevant tier
   - Pilot options
   - Board presentation support

**Preparation Required:**
- Research library's catalog
- Customize examples to their community
- Prepare ROI calculations
- Have contracts ready

**Conversion Goal:** 40% to pilot/purchase

### Demo Tier 3: On-Site Proof of Concept

**Format:** Half-day or full-day on-site visit

**Audience:** Large libraries, consortiums, or those requiring stakeholder buy-in

**Content:**
- Morning: Staff workshop and training
- Afternoon: Board/stakeholder presentation
- Technical integration review with IT
- Hands-on testing by staff

**When to Offer:**
- Contract value >$30K
- Consortium opportunity
- Strategic account (influence potential)

**Investment:**
- Drew's time + travel costs
- Justified by contract value and influence

**Conversion Goal:** 60% to purchase

### Demo Scheduling Strategy

**Availability Windows:**
- Tier 1: Daily slots (can batch 4 per hour)
- Tier 2: Tues/Wed/Thurs (best attendance)
- Tier 3: Flexible based on opportunity

**Pre-Demo Requirements:**
- Send agenda 24 hours before
- Confirm attendees and roles
- Request ILS credentials if possible
- Share one-pager for internal circulation

**Post-Demo Follow-Up:**
- Same-day thank you with recording
- 48-hour follow-up with proposal
- 1-week check-in if no response
- 2-week final follow-up

## Pricing FAQs & Objection Handling

### "What does it cost?"

**Response Framework:**
"Our Professional tier, which most libraries your size choose, is $18,995 annually for both Scout and Finch. This includes [specific benefits]. To put that in perspective, it's less than half the cost of one library assistant, but provides 24/7 support for both patrons and staff. Should I send you detailed pricing for all tiers?"

### "We don't have budget"

**Response Options:**
1. **Grant Angle:** "Many libraries use LSTA or state grants. We can provide language for your application."
2. **Pilot Angle:** "We offer a 3-month pilot at $2,374 to prove ROI before full commitment."
3. **Timing Angle:** "When does your new fiscal year begin? We can time implementation accordingly."

### "We just bought [Competitor]"

**Response Framework:**
"That's great that you're investing in [specific area]. Scout and Finch actually complement [Competitor] by [specific differentiation]. [Library X] uses both and finds they serve different needs. When does your contract renewal come up? We could time a pilot to help inform that decision."

### "Send me information"

**Response Framework:**
"I'd be happy to send relevant information. To make sure I send what's most useful - are you most interested in patron discovery, staff efficiency, or both? And what's your timeline for making improvements in this area?"

[Wait for answer, then...]

"Based on what you've shared, I'll send our [specific resource]. Would you have 15 minutes this week for a quick call to make sure I'm addressing your specific situation?"

### "We need board approval"

**Response Framework:**
"Absolutely, board buy-in is critical. We support that process in three ways:
1. ROI documentation specific to your library
2. Reference calls with peer libraries
3. We can present directly to your board if helpful

## Payment Terms & Policies

### Standard Terms

**Payment Options:**
- Annual in advance (standard)
- Semi-annual (add 3%)
- Quarterly (add 5%)

**Payment Methods:**
- ACH/Wire (preferred)
- Check (Net 60)

- Purchase Order (for government entities)

### Flexible Terms for Public Libraries

**Extended Payment Terms:**
- Net 60 standard
- Net 90 for amounts >$25K
- Can accept POs for full amount

**Fiscal Year Accommodation:**
- Pro-rating for mid-year starts
- Deferred billing to new fiscal year
- Split invoicing across fiscal years

### Consortium/Group Pricing

**Volume Discounts:**
- 3-5 libraries: 10% discount
- 6-10 libraries: 20% discount
- 11-20 libraries: 30% discount
- 20+ libraries: Custom pricing

**Consortium Benefits:**
- Single point of contact
- Consolidated invoicing
- Shared implementation costs
