# Finch: AI-Powered Librarian Assistant
*Empowering Library Staff to Focus on What Matters Most*

---

## Executive Summary

Finch is an intelligent assistant designed specifically for library staff, addressing the #1 challenge librarians face: "not enough time to do everything." By automating routine tasks, streamlining multi-system workflows, and providing 24/7 onboarding support, Finch helps your team focus on complex patron services, programming, and community engagement.

**The Crisis Finch Addresses:**
- Librarians report insufficient staffing as their top challenge (per context research)
- New staff typically take 6-8 weeks to become productive
- Librarians must switch between multiple different systems daily
- Many reference questions are repetitive "where/how" queries
- Evening and weekend shifts often lack senior staff support

**Finch's Intended Impact:**
- Significant time savings on routine tasks
- Faster staff onboarding and productivity
- Reduction in repetitive reference questions
- Self-service policy and procedure lookups
- Improved patron satisfaction through consistent service

## Core Product Capabilities

### 1. Unified Multi-System Search Dashboard
**What It Is:** One interface to search across ILS, OverDrive, databases, and internal resources simultaneously.

**Systems Integrated:**
- ILS catalog with staff-level permissions
- OverDrive/Libby administrative portal
- Hoopla management interface
- Database administration
- Internal knowledge base and policies

**Staff Workflow Benefits:**
- No more tab switching between systems
- Single search finds materials across all platforms
- See patron's complete history and holds in one view
- Bulk operations across systems

**Real Use Example:**
Patron asks: "Do you have the new Louise Penny in any format?"
Traditional way: Check ILS, log into OverDrive, check Hoopla separately, Check WorldCat for inter-library loans
With Finch: One search shows all formats and availability instantly, while also recommends other complimentary materials

**Why This Matters:** Reduces context-switching fatigue and accelerates patron service. Provides patron with a wealth of free materials beyond just their singular item request. 

### 2. One-Click Cross-Platform Actions
**What It Is:** Perform patron account actions across multiple systems without separate logins.

**Capabilities:**
- Place holds across ILS and OverDrive simultaneously
- Renew items regardless of source system
- Update patron information across all platforms
- Cancel holds and manage waitlists
- Process interlibrary loans
- Register patrons for programs
- Provide recommendations to explore more library materials to complement, enhance, and elevate their knowledge discovery needs

**Process Acceleration:**
- Multi-system hold placement becomes single-click
- Patron account updates across all platforms at once
- ILL processing streamlined into fewer steps

**Real Use Example:**
Patron wants next book in series available in any format:
- Finch shows book 4 available physically, book 5 as ebook
- One click places both holds
- Automatically suggests audiobook for book 6 via ILL

**Why This Matters:** Eliminates the frustration of repetitive logins and duplicate data entry across systems.
