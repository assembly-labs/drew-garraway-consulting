==============================================
SCORING CRITERIA (1-10)
==============================================
10 points total based on:
- ILS Match (3 points): Polaris/Koha=3, Sierra=2, Other=1
- Size Fit (3 points): 25-75K=3, 15-25K or 75-100K=2, Other=1
- Innovation Signs (2 points): Recent grant/tech=2, Some=1, None=0
- Geography (2 points): PA/DE=2, Priority state=1, Other=0

==============================================
STATUS DEFINITIONS
==============================================
Cold: Not contacted yet
Contacted: Outreach sent, no response
Qualified: Responded with interest
Discovery: Call scheduled/completed
Demo: Demo scheduled/completed
Proposal: Proposal/agreement sent
Negotiating: Discussing terms
Won: Joined cohort
Lost: Declined/Not now
Nurture: Not ready but stay in touch
