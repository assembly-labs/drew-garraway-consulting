### Museum Passport Program
**PUNCH CARDS**

1. Reserve one of Tredyffrin's museum passes
2. Visit the attraction
3. Receive a punch on your card at Tredyffrin's Reference Desk. Stop by the Desk before you return your physical pass to show proof of attendance.
4. Complete four punches and return your card to the Reference Desk for a prize.
5. The Longwood Gardens vouchers is valid for up to four (4) free general admission tickets and 25% off of a Membership. It's for a one-time use before September 30, 2025.

**Please Note: Prizes are limited! Once we distribute all of our Longwood Gardens vouchers, we will not be getting more. After September 30, 2025, we will be introducing a new prize.**

Questions? Contact the Reference Desk at 610-688-7092 x212
