# NOW streaming: Thoughtful Entertainment

## What is Kanopy?

Kanopy is a video streaming service that provides instant access to critically acclaimed movies and documentaries. We partnered with Kanopy to bring you their unique collection of thoughtful entertainment.

## How can I watch Kanopy?

You can stream Kanopy on any of your favorite devices! Kanopy is accessible via web tools on all tablets (ipods for iPads and Android), phones (iOS and Android), Apple TV, and Fire Stick.

## How to create an account:

To get started, visit ccls.kanopy.com and select the "Add Library Card" button to enter the library card information and create an account. Or, download the Kanopy app to your preferred email.

### Process:
1. A patron will pick up a blank punch card at the Reference Desk.
2. A patron will reserve a museum pass. They may ask for your help to do so!
3. A patron will pick up the museum pass from the circulation desk. If needed,
4. A patron will return the Reference Desk with their museum pass (enter the physical card) on the printable) to show proof of visiting the attraction.
5. You will punch their card.
6. Keep the punch card at the Reference Desk. When complete, the voucher may be used by September 30, 2025. These vouchers are kept in a labeled envelope on the shelf behind the Desk.

***There will be quarter sheets listing the rules available at the Desk for patrons.
