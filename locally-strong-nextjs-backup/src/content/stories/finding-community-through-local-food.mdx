On a random Saturday, I wandered into the Wayne Farmers Market mostly out of boredom. I left three hours later with a bag of peaches, a jar of local honey, and conversations with four different vendors.

I started going every week. The vegetable vendor learned my name. The bread baker saved me a loaf of sourdough. The flower farmer started recommending arrangements for my bare apartment.
