# The Mobile App

## What can you do with the Chester County Library System's Mobile App?

- Check out books with your digital library card
- Search for books, movies, and other items
- Put materials on hold
- Renew items
- Find library events
- ... and so much more!

## Follow these steps to download the Mobile App:

1. Go to the App Store or Play Store on your device.
2. Search for "Chester County Library System". The icon should look similar to this:
   [Chester County Library System logo]
3. Once the app is downloaded to the device, open it and click "My Account".
4. Click "Manage Accounts".
5. Enter your Chester County Library System's card number.
6. Enter your 4-digit PIN (personal identification number). If you do not have a PIN, follow the instructions on the other side of this page.
7. You are ready to start exploring!
