## Key Findings

### The 90-Second Window
Users have approximately 90 seconds of willingness to log post-training. After that, friction wins.

### Dropout Crisis
- 80-90% quit in first 6 months
- 60-70% of blue belts quit after promotion
- First 90 days predict long-term retention

### Self-Regulation = Skill Acquisition
Self-regulation processes predict **90% of variance** in athletic skill performance. Tracking works.

### Two Touchpoints = 33% Less Churn
Two staff interactions per month reduces gym member cancellations by 33%.
