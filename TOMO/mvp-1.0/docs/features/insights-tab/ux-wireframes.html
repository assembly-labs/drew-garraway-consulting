Design Notes

The typewriter reveals text at 20ms/char with a 12-character gold glow trail. A pulsing gold cursor marks the leading edge. "Skip" in the header jumps to full reveal. Chat input fades in only after text completes. Quarterly/monthly cards are collapsed by default -- tap to expand (typewriter on first view only). Subsequent visits show static text, no re-animation.

Design Notes

No tab bar change -- still 3 tabs. Insight cards use gold/white left borders to stand apart from session cards. The core risk is visible in the "Day 14" wireframe: after a busy week, the debrief sits below 4 session entries. Users who open the Journal, see their latest session, and close the app will never scroll down to it. The monthly card has more visual weight (larger padding, white border) but still competes with session entries for attention.
