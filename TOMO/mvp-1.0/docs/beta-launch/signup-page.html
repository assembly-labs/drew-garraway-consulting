FAQ
Do I need an iPhone?
Yes. TOMO is distributed through Apple TestFlight, which requires an iPhone running iOS 16 or later. Android is not supported during the beta.
Is it really free?
Completely free during the beta. When TOMO launches publicly it will be a paid subscription, but beta testers from Alliance Paoli will get a heavily discounted rate as a thank-you.
Is my data private?
Yes. Your training data is yours. Nobody else at the gym can see your sessions, notes, or stats. Not your coach, not your training partners.
How much time does it take after each class?
About 90 seconds. You talk through your session naturally and TOMO handles the rest. It's designed for when you're exhausted and just want to get to your car.
How do I report bugs or give feedback?
Email drew@assemblylabs.co or grab Drew at the gym. A quick text or voice note about what happened is perfect.
