12px Minimum Font Size

Nothing in the app should be smaller than 12px. Our users are exhausted post-training with reduced cognitive capacity. Text smaller than 12px becomes illegible on mobile.

Weight Comparison: Why 500+ Matters

On dark backgrounds, thin fonts lose contrast. Weight 500 (medium) is the minimum for body text. Our exhausted post-training users need maximum legibility.
