Recommendation

Top picks: Clash Display or Satoshi for hero numbers and headlines, paired with Inter for body text. Both offer the modern, thick aesthetic TOMO needs while maintaining legibility.

Clash Display has more personality and trend-forward appeal. Satoshi is more versatile with its full weight range (up to 900) and works well at all sizes.

Consider: Use the display font only for hero numbers (144px+) and major headlines (32px+). Keep Inter for everything else to maintain the proven legibility your exhausted users need.
