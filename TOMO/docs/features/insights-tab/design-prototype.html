White Belt Caught You
Getting caught by a lower belt stings at blue. But it happens to everyone -- including purple belts who won't admit it. The triangle they caught you with tells you something about your posture in their guard. That's useful data, not a failure.
Technique
Same Core, Deeper Grooves
Half guard, closed guard, arm drags -- same as last month. That's not stagnation at blue belt. That's depth. New techniques are exciting but depth in your existing game is what works under pressure.
Focus Next Week
Next open mat, start every round from your worst position. If you survive longer than you did two months ago, that's progress the numbers won't show you.

Design Token Accuracy

Every color, font, size, and spacing value in this prototype matches the production design-tokens.ts file exactly. Background #111111, cards #1f1f1f, borders #4a4a4a, body text #c4c4c4, labels #a3a3a3, gold #F5A623. Type badges use the exact dim/full color pairs from the token file. The tab bar matches MainTabNavigator.tsx (88px height, 28px bottom padding, 70px gold center button with 32px float). Font families match: Unbounded-ExtraBold for titles, Inter for body, JetBrains Mono for labels.

What's new vs. existing: Collapsed week rows (cw component) are new -- the current InsightsScreen renders all weeks expanded. The TrendUp tab icon is ported from the web prototype (Lucide MIT lineart). Everything else matches the existing InsightsScreen.tsx styles line-for-line.
