Journal Entry Card Variations

Card Content by Belt

  • JRNL-CARD-WHITE - Date, type, duration, lesson topic, notes (simplified)
  • JRNL-CARD-BLUE - Adds: techniques drilled, sparring rounds, what worked/struggled
  • JRNL-CARD-PURPLE - Adds: submissions given/received breakdown

Empty State

  • JRNL-EMPTY - "No sessions logged yet" with CTA to log first session
  • Encouraging illustration or icon
  • Primary action: "Log Your First Session" button

Session Detail Sections

Detail View Sections (DTAIL-*)

  • DTAIL-HERO - AI Narrative: Typewriter-style summary of the session
  • DTAIL-META - Training Details: Type, duration, date/time
  • DTAIL-TECH - Techniques: List of drilled techniques
  • DTAIL-SPAR - Sparring: Partners, belts, round outcomes
  • DTAIL-INSIGHT - Insights: What worked well / what I struggled with
  • DTAIL-SUBS - Submissions: Given / Received
  • DTAIL-MOOD - Energy & Mood: Rating sliders
  • DTAIL-NOTES - Notes: Free-form session notes

Edit Sheet Modals

Bottom Sheet Edit Components (EDIT-*)

  • EDIT-TRAINING - Training Details: Type selector, duration picker, round count
  • EDIT-TECH - Techniques: Add/remove technique chips
  • EDIT-INSIGHT - Insights: Worked well / Struggled text areas
  • EDIT-NOTES - Notes: Session notes textarea
  • EDIT-MOOD - Energy & Mood: Slider editing
  • EDIT-SPAR - Sparring: Round detail editing

Edit Sheet Behavior

  • Opens as bottom sheet modal (80% screen height)
  • Staged changes - requires Save or Discard
  • Large touch targets for exhausted users
  • Tap outside or swipe down to close
