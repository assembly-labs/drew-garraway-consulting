Insight States

INS-READY

New session logged. "Generate Insight" button available. Shows session summary that will be analyzed.

INS-GENERATING

Typewriter animation in progress. Loading indicator. "Analyzing your training..." message.

INS-COMPLETE

Full insight displayed with AI-generated headline, coaching text, focus areas, and recommended videos.

INS-ALREADY

"You've already generated today's insight. Come back tomorrow!" with the previous insight still visible.

INS-EMPTY

"Log some sessions first to get personalized insights." CTA to log a session.

Insight Components

Component Reference

  • INS-TITLE - AI-generated headline (e.g., "Your Guard Game is Evolving")
  • INS-BODY - Coaching text with specific observations and advice
  • INS-FOCUS - Focus areas: 2-3 technique recommendations
  • INS-VIDEO - Recommended videos section linking to TechniqueLibrary

Belt-Adaptive Tone

  • White: Encouraging, consistency-focused, "Every session counts"
  • Blue: Game development, identity building, "Developing your style"
  • Purple: Technical depth, teaching perspective, "Refining your approach"
  • Brown: Mastery refinement, legacy thinking, "Polishing the details"

At-Risk Persona Messaging

  • Detects gap in training frequency
  • Acknowledges struggle without judgment
  • Provides "When It Gets Hard" support content
  • Links to mindset videos (mental game, motivation)
