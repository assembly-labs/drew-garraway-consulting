Video Components

Video Card Components

  • LIB-VIDEO-HERO - Large featured video card (full width, 16:9 thumbnail)
  • LIB-VIDEO-COMPACT - Compact list card (thumbnail + title + duration)
  • LIB-VIDEO-PLAYER - Full-screen YouTube embed modal

Video Card Elements

  • YouTube thumbnail (with fallback chain)
  • Duration badge overlay
  • Title (2 lines max)
  • Channel name
  • Belt level badge (if applicable)

Personalization Factors

  • Belt level determines content complexity
  • Recent struggles surface relevant technique videos
  • At-risk personas see mindset/motivation content
  • Training gaps surface remedial content
