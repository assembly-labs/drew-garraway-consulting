TOMO

Privacy Policy — Last updated March 8, 2026

Overview

TOMO ("the App") is a BJJ training journal that helps you log sessions via voice or text. Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What We Collect

  • Account info: Email address (for authentication).
  • Profile data: Name, belt rank, gym name, training preferences. You provide this during onboarding.
  • Session data: Training logs you create, including techniques, sparring details, energy/mood ratings, and notes.
  • Voice recordings: Audio recorded when you use voice logging. Recordings are processed for transcription and then stored securely. They are never shared.
  • Device info: Crash reports via Sentry (device model, OS version, stack traces). No personal data is included in crash reports.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the core journaling experience (storing and displaying your training sessions).
  • To transcribe voice recordings into text using AssemblyAI (a third-party transcription service).
  • To extract structured session data from transcripts using AI (Anthropic Claude).
  • To fix bugs and improve app stability via crash reports.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Database and authentication. Data stored in the US. Privacy Policy
  • AssemblyAI: Voice transcription. Audio is processed and not retained. Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic: AI text processing. Transcripts are processed and not retained. Privacy Policy
  • Sentry: Crash reporting. No personal data collected. Privacy Policy

Data Storage & Security

All data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security policies. Voice recordings are stored in a private bucket accessible only to the recording user. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. We do not sell or share your data with advertisers.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You can delete individual sessions at any time. To delete your entire account and all associated data, contact us at the email below.

Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time.
  • Export your session data (feature coming soon).
  • Delete your account by contacting us.

Children

TOMO is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
