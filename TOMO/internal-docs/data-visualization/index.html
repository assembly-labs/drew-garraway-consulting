Implementation Notes

Advanced Visualizations

These visualizations require more complex data or have mobile optimization challenges. Consider for desktop or future phases.

Position Transition Flow (Sankey)

Shows position sequences during rolling. Powerful for strategic planning but complex on mobile. Confidence: 5. Consider desktop-only implementation.

Coach Feedback Theme Timeline

Stream graph showing how feedback themes evolve over time. Requires NLP categorization of feedback text. Confidence: 6. May simplify to stacked bar chart.

Technique Drilling Frequency

Word cloud or bubble chart showing drilling distribution. Word clouds are trendy but hard to read on mobile. Bubble charts more reliable. Confidence: 7.

Injury Timeline & Recovery

Critical for safety tracking. 66% of BJJ athletes injured within 3 years. Implement as dedicated section, not main dashboard. Confidence: 8.
